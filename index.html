About Me

My name is Eamon O’Connor, and I hope to improve people’s lives through a career in bioinformatics.

I have academic and professional experience in Philosophy, Civil Engineering, and Veterinary Medicine, but discovered a passion for bioinformatics during the last year of my undergraduate studies. I believe that this path presents an alignment between my interests and talents that will allow me to make a positive impact that is both far-reaching and significant in the lives of individuals.

My experience thus far includes analysis of Next Generation Sequencing data and studies on how biological systems are affected by disease, however I hope to explore vaccine or drug discovery in the next steps of my career.

Outside of work I enjoy reading, travelling, and cooking. I am currently based in the Greater Boston area, but am actively exploring opportunities in California and overseas.

If you have any inquiries, comments, or questions, don’t hesitate to reach out!
